Blackjack

Rules


Objective

Each participant attempts to beat the dealer by getting a count as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.

Card Values / Scoring

A (worth 11 or 1), K, Q, J (face cards worth 10), 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2.

How To Play
